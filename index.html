We leave Salt Lake City

This is it, the day we are leaving Salt Lake City. The truck is packed (we did this the night before) and we are ready to hit the road. Did we hire professional movers? No, we are going to do this ourselves. We have a Penske™ truck, two boys, and a cat and this is going to be great.

10 Minuets later we break down.

Well the title says it all. After being on the road for only 10 Minuets we break down. AAs it turns out the steering tires on the Penske™ truck we rented needed replacing. We were not even out of the valley we started in and the repair took 5 hours for someone to show up and get us going. Needless to say my wife was less than pleased.

We are moving.

Yes, Miracles do happen. Ours came in the form of a working Penske™ truck. At this point it is about 1:30 pm and we drive for about 3 hours. As a side note this trip may sound cursed and it was. This is the point in the story my phone melts it's charcging port.

SO after 4 hours of driving we make it to Price, UT.

We make it to Price UT a middle of nowhere town that is well placed. We eat dinner and tell our boys that they can swim if they are good. we finally get some sleep and the cat gets a little of time out of the truck. But have no fear we get some rest and a fresh start the next day.

The next day goes great.

Were moving and chewing up the miles. The day is smooth if you don't count the kids being kids. We leave Utah and make it to Colorado. As soon as we are in the state the terain changes and the mountains are uppon us. This part of the drive includes sharp curves, tunnels through mountains and steep climbs/decents.

Denver here we, Ok Bye Denver.

We didn't stop here and we were past denver in about 20 mins. I think it helps you get a great "Push from the mountains".

I'm Tired lets sleep in Burlington, CO.

A few hours after Denver we make it to Burlington, CO where we decide sleep in a bed is better than sleeping while driving. We get a room at the Best Western. Another day down (and at this point we are worn out so it seemed like) a million days to go.

A new Day a new State Kansas.

The last day, we are close, I can feel it. The only thing standing in our way is 430 miles of wind, cows, windmills, and farms. There are worst ways to die check Google if you dont believe me.

We made it Kansas City, MO. Hey was that a pearly gate?

Three full days later and we learned a lesson. They make planes for a reason. Not quite like the Oregon Trail but close. Now that we are here the real work begins, putting together a new house. It was a memorable experience but would I do it again? No and No.
